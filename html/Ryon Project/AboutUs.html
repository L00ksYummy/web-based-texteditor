About Us:
Supporting Children & Families Through Life’s Challenges Ryon Family Wellness specializes in compassionate, evidence-based mental health therapy for children, adolescents, and families navigating the complexities of life. Whether you're facing the daily hurdles of ADHD, working through parent-child conflict, or coping with anxiety, depression, or trauma, we are here to walk alongside you with care and expertise.


We understand that every family’s story is unique. That’s why we offer tailored support for:
  • ADHD & Behavioral Challenges: Helping children build focus, emotional regulation, and confidence.
  • Parent-Child Relationships: Strengthening communication and connection through family-centered therapy.
  • Anxiety & Depression: Providing tools to manage overwhelming emotions and foster resilience.
  • Kinship Care & Out-of-Home Placement: Supporting caregivers and children through transitions, grief, and identity development.
  • Trauma Recovery: Creating safe spaces for healing from abuse, neglect, or loss.
  • Transitioning to Adulthood: Guiding teens and young adults through identity, independence, and future planning.
Our therapists are trained in trauma-informed care, play therapy, cognitive-behavioral approaches, and family systems work. We believe in empowering families with practical strategies, emotional insight, and a renewed sense of hope. Whether you're seeking support for your child, your family, or yourself—we’re here to help you thrive, not just survive.
